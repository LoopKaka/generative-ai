Step-Back Prompting:
====================
Prompt:
Before answering the userâ€™s question, first break down the problem into smaller reasoning steps. Think carefully about the underlying concepts, context, or requirements. Only after analyzing these steps, provide a structured answer.

Example:
User: How can I start a small business with limited capital?
Step-Back Reasoning:
1. Identify your skills and interests.
2. Research business ideas that require minimal investment.
3. Understand local regulations and resources.
4. Plan initial budget and revenue model.
Answer: Based on these steps, you could start a service-based business like freelancing, tutoring, or online reselling.
